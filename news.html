9.23.14

The Batch Yard

Markley's neon artwork Half-naked, purchased at the 19th Annual ARTWALK NY Coalition for the Homeless Benefit, found its way to The Batch Yard project in Everett, MA. It is installed in the bar area and overlooks the pool, placed to delight with its cheek, irreverence, and fun.

Half-naked references a scene in Blue Movie by Andy Warhol, an afternoon in a confined interior (a bedroom). The two actors, Viva and Louis Waldron, talk about insects, athlete’s foot, Vietnam, etc, while playing with a metal Indian belt.
