Norma Markley

Neon

Pom-Pom Cloud series, 19 x 19 x 3 in. (unless specified). Editions of 3

Pom-Pom Clouds are drawings in white bright neon; minimal line, flat space, placement varied. "This art is happy (it is a happy art) to contemplate," wrote Donald Judd in a catalog essay for John Wesley's PS1 exhibit. Apply this to Markley's neon pieces. They are meant to catch one between everyday life and a world of dreams. Above, below, illuminated.
